About

Who are we?

We are working on producing a healthy date seed spread, completely free of hydrogenated ingredients, with 50% less sugar than any other spread on the market. We have crafted a healthy alternative packed with nutrients and balanced nutrition without compromising on taste.

How does the manufacturing process work?

  • 1 Date seed collecting from date factories.
  • 2 Date seed grinding & roasting.
  • 3 Spread manufacturing.
  • 4 Spread packaging & distributing.
